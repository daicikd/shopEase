Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for select items.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the weight of your order. Free shipping is available for orders over $50.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How do I initiate a return?

Contact our customer service team through your account dashboard or email support@shopease.com.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other popular payment methods.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.

Do you store my credit card information?

No, we do not store your credit card information. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order through your account dashboard or using the tracking number sent to your email.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. Contact customer service for assistance.

How do I update my account information?

You can update your information through your account settings or profile page.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic and sourced from authorized manufacturers.

Do you offer product warranties?

Most products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty information is listed on product pages.

What if I receive a damaged item?

Contact our customer service immediately with photos of the damaged item for assistance.
